NEGATIVE EFFECTS OF LACK SELF-CONFIDENCE

Negative Feelings

Confidence and self-esteem are closely related to our behavior and moods. So when the confidence in you is diminished, you might experience a slew of negative thoughts such as shame, loneliness, anxiety, fear, anger, rage and even depression

Self-Loathing

Lack of confidence can affect the self-love. In some cases, it could make you hate yourself. Many people have difficulty in forgiving themselves for making mistakes

Lack of Self-Care

Lack of confidence directly affects your self-worth. People with a lack of confidence think that there is not any way to get things better. That is why they ignore essential elements of life such as sleep, healthy diet, exercise, self care, etc
