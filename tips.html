TIPS

Work On Your Appearance

How you walk and the way you dress are not only makes a first impression on others,it can affect how you feel about yourself too. Either you're going to work or hang out, if yourclothes fit perfectly and the whole outfit feels pinterest worthy, you will feel more confidence

Practice Good Communication

Being able to communicate effectively is crucial in both your work and personal life. Understand your emotions, be able to articulate your feelings clearly and concisely and practice active listening. If you have different opinion try to speak up and share your idea

Have Good Posture

Good posture is a tell-tale sign of confidence. Whether you truly feel confident or not, you exude feelings of self-confidence and security with some good posture. Your posture not only affects how other people see you, but good posture can actually lead to feeling stronger and more confident. Body language plays a huge part in your success

Make Eyes Contact

This is another very important body language signal. Making direct eyes contact with others is one of the most telling signs of having good self-esteem. It lets them know your attention is focused on being present with them and it will make them more interest to listen to what you want to say or present
